# The Convent — Mission Statement (Draft v0)

The Convent is a space where the most creative people in the world come together to build, share, and teach at the frontier of art and technology. We believe we are living through a renaissance — one where the barriers between idea and creation have collapsed, and the tools to express yourself are no longer controlled by gatekeepers. Our mission is to equip artists, technologists, and curious people with open, powerful tools that turn them from passive consumers into active creators. Rooted in Brooklyn and radiating outward, we cultivate shared intention around a future that is expressive, participatory, and human — not one dictated to us by corporations or algorithms. We exist to make people feel something they haven't felt in a long time: that the future is theirs to build, together.
