## Concept

"Sound Sculptures" is an immersive exhibition exploring every way sound can be visualized, felt, and experienced beyond the ear. Attendees walk through a series of installations, live demos, and interactive stations that transform audio into visual, tactile, and spatial experiences.

---

## Visualization Stations / Installations

### 1. Cymatics Lab
- Chladni plates with tone generators showing sand/particle patterns
- Water cymatics (speaker-driven standing waves in shallow dishes)
- Cornstarch on subwoofers (non-Newtonian fluid dancing)

### 2. Oscilloscope Art & Lissajous Figures
- XY-mode oscilloscopes generating real-time audio visuals
- Vintage analog scopes alongside modern digital displays
- Live musicians feeding audio into scopes

### 3. Spectrogram Wall
- Large-format real-time spectrogram projections
- Audience can speak/sing and see their voice signatures
- Historical spectrograms of famous recordings

### 4. LED Sound Reactive Sculptures
- Physical sculptures with embedded LED arrays responding to audio
- Frequency-band-mapped color displays
- Could tie into Nouriel installation tech (projector mapping)

### 5. Laser Audio Visualization
- Laser galvanometers driven by audio signals
- Smoke/haze environment for beam visibility
- Safety-compliant laser show setup

### 6. 3D Printed Sound
- Physical 3D prints of waveforms and frequency spectra
- Live demo of slicing audio into printable geometry
- Take-home mini sound sculptures from attendees' voices

### 7. Tactile/Haptic Sound
- Subpac-style tactile transducer vests
- Vibration platforms (feel the bass)
- Bone conduction listening stations

### 8. AI-Driven Visualizations
- Real-time AI generating visuals from live audio input
- Style-transfer from sound to image
- Interactive station where attendees create their own

### 9. Spatial Audio Dome / Sweet Spot
- Ambisonics or wave field synthesis demo
- Binaural recording playback station
- Sound spatialization visualization showing speaker arrays

### 10. Analog Synth Visualization Playground
- Modular synths patched into visual outputs
- Attendees can patch and play
- Waveform education: sine, square, saw, triangle
