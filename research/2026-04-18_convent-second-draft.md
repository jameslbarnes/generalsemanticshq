# THE CONVENT

**Sovereignty. Expression. Agency.**

The Convent is a renaissance accelerator that explores the use of frontier technologies to amplify human sovereignty, agency, and expression in the intelligence age. Our community of engineers, artists, hackers, and founders is seeking collaborators across all disciplines to join us on this mission from our 20,000 square foot urban campus in the heart of Greenpoint, Brooklyn.

We're living in an extraordinary moment. The promises of utopia exist just as credibly as the perils of dystopia. Boundless progress across nearly all disciplines is mentioned in the same breath as unprecedented erosion of privacy, rapid centralization of power, and predatory exploitation of human attention. We are united by our belief in our collective agency to shape the future we want to live in together, and propelled by the values we care about.

We build open source creative tools and put them in the hands of people who have something to say. We host residencies, salons, and collaborative R&D across art, technology, music, science, and whatever else walks through the door. Our flagship project, Etherea, is a real-time AI platform that transforms speech, movement, and sound into immersive projected environments. It is software that listens.

We believe the most important work happens when people who are excellent at what they do have access to tools that are truly fit for them. Not tools designed to extract their attention or monetize their creativity, but tools that extend their capabilities and belong to them. We build those tools together, we share them freely, and we teach people how to build their own.

The Convent is part studio, part lab, part stage. A research and production space outside the constraints of legacy institutions, where small teams of exceptional people move fast, iterate in the open, and produce work that would not be possible anywhere else.

We are assembling the founding community now.

## FOUNDING MEMBERS (30)

Thirty high-agency polymaths at the top of their crafts. Builders and performers, researchers and writers, technologists and teachers, often several at once. People with the taste to see what the moment demands and the conviction to build it. Members get a home inside the Convent, a seat at everything that happens here, and the one thing you cannot buy: the right collaborators.

## FOUNDING TEAMS (2)

Two small teams, 10 to 20 people each, doing work we believe in. Private offices inside a former parochial school. A rooftop with one of the best views in Brooklyn. A building full of people who will sharpen what you're building simply by being nearby. Teams making tools for human agency, expression, or sovereignty are strongly preferred, but the filter that matters most is whether the work is any good.

## FOUNDING SPONSORS

A small, hand-picked circle of institutions and individuals helping us build the Convent into what it can be, through capital, collaboration, or simply showing up at the right moments with the right people. Sponsors fund the programming, the research, and the people who make this possible, and in exchange get a front-row seat to one of the most interesting experiments in New York. We are in conversation with a few.

---

## VALUES

**Sovereignty.** Your tools, your data, your creative output belong to you. We build open systems that empower people rather than extract from them. In a moment when power is centralizing fast, self-determination is not a feature. It's the foundation.

**Expression.** We exist to close the gap between idea and creation. The ability to make something that moves people is no longer reserved for those with access to studios, distributors, or gatekeepers. We champion people who have something to say and give them the means to say it.

**Agency.** The future is not something that happens to us. We cultivate shared intention around the world we want to live in and build the tools and communities to make it real. In a time when many people feel the ground shifting beneath them, we offer a place to take action, contribute, and feel ownership over what comes next.

SHORT SUMMARY (business):

The Convent is a production research facility and creative accelerator in Brooklyn operating at the intersection of live performance, real-time AI, and open source tooling. Founded by artists and technologists, we provide the resources, platform, and community for exceptional creatives to expand their practice using frontier technology.

Our flagship platform, Etherea, is a real-time orchestration engine for live AI models that transforms speech, movement, and sound into immersive projected environments. We partner with leading cultural institutions and enterprise production infrastructure providers to deploy these capabilities at scale.

The space supports residencies, collaborative R&D, live production, and education. We build open source tools, share them freely, and teach people how to use them and how to build their own. Our programs bring together artists, engineers, researchers, and performers across disciplines to prototype, produce, and distribute work that would not be possible anywhere else.

We are strategic in our research, innovative without fear, collaborative across cultures, compassionate in how we treat people, and relentlessly positive about what's possible when you put the right people in the same room with the right tools.
